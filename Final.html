Creating Data Frames for Each Year

The crux of the analysis revolves around a comparison of the most popular words that appear in newspaper headlines versus the most popular words that appear in song titles from 1996 to the present. In order to do this, a Shiny App will be created so that filtering may be done based on year and source of the most popular words used.

Year by Year Comparison of Album Titles and NYT Headlines

The Shiny App offers users an interactive way to filter based on the most popular words that appear in song titles and newspapers headlines from 1996 to the present. The widget offers the ability to filter on specific years as well as specific sources (Million Songs database versus NY Times headlines).
